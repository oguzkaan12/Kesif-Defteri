Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Keşif Defteri ("the App") is developed by OğuzK App Studio. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use it.

About Keşif Defteri

Keşif Defteri is a nature discovery application designed to help users identify, learn about, and record plants, insects, animals, fungi, and other species found in nature.

The App may provide species photographs, descriptions, habitat information, interesting facts, and region-specific species databases that can be downloaded for offline use.

Information We Collect

Location Information

The App may request access to your location to show species and discovery content relevant to your selected or current region. Location access is optional and can be disabled at any time through your device settings.

Camera and Photos

The App may request access to your camera or photo gallery to allow you to photograph species, save observations, or add images to your discoveries. Photos remain under your control and are not shared unless you choose to do so.

Device and Usage Information

The App may collect limited technical information such as device type, operating system version, app version, crash reports, and diagnostics. This information is used to improve app performance, stability, and user experience.

Offline Species Databases

To improve usability in remote areas without internet access, the App may allow users to download regional species databases for offline use.

Downloaded species databases may include:

  • Species names
  • Species photographs
  • Scientific classifications
  • Species descriptions
  • Habitat information
  • Educational content

These databases are stored locally on your device and can be removed at any time by deleting downloaded content or uninstalling the App.

User Discoveries

The App may allow users to save personal discoveries, observations, notes, photographs, and location information associated with species they encounter.

By default, these records remain on your device and are not publicly shared.

Future versions of the App may introduce optional cloud synchronization or community features. Any such changes will be reflected in updates to this Privacy Policy.

How We Use Information

  • To provide nature discovery and species information features.
  • To show region-based species data.
  • To save user preferences and downloaded data locally.
  • To improve app stability and fix bugs.
  • To display advertisements if ads are enabled.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Information may only be shared when required by law, to protect legal rights, or through third-party services necessary for app functionality.

Children's Privacy

Keşif Defteri is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.

Your Choices

  • You can disable location, camera, and photo permissions from your device settings.
  • You can clear locally stored app data.
  • You can uninstall the App at any time.
  • You can contact us regarding privacy concerns.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information handled by the App. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Purpose

Keşif Defteri is intended for educational, scientific, and recreational nature exploration purposes.
